Test 1 (16 Personalities):



Graph 1.1 -16 Personalities Results

As displayed in Graph 1.1, two of our group members have the personality type advocate, the advantage of having advocates in a group situation is advantageous as they always “find ways to make nearly any position work” (Workplace Habits | Advocate (INFJ) Personality | 16Personalities, 2021). In a group dynamic Advocates “gravitate toward managers who are open-minded" (Workplace Habits | Advocate (INFJ) Personality | 16Personalities, 2021), therefore it is important that the individual and managers morals align.



Groups seeks contains two members with the Logician Personality type, unlike the advocate personality type logicians “logician personalities prefer to work alone” (Workplace Habits | Logician (INTP) Personality | 16Personalities, 2021) and can view their colleagues as “a series of obstacles” (Workplace Habits | Logician (INTP). Although logicians can be tolerant as leaders, they “do best with a partner” (Workplace Habits | Logician (INTP).



The team leader of group seeks has the architect personality type. As a subordinate “Architects are known for their independence” and will find it difficult to work under a “Micro-managing” boss (Workplace Habits | Architect (INTJ) Personality | 16Personalities, 2021). In a management position, individuals with this personality type “make great leaders”. This is because they disregard common hierarchies and “promote innovation and effectiveness” (Workplace Habits | Architect (INTJ) Personality | 16Personalities, 2021).

The information detailed above may be helpful for our group members as it provides some insight on the individuals in our groups and how to best interact with them. With the right leader knowing ones’ likelihood of behavior in a workplace or group dynamic can result in a positive group experience.
